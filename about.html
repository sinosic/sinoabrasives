About Sino Abrasives

Our Company

Sino Abrasives Co., Ltd. is a professional manufacturer and exporter of abrasive materials, located in Haizhou District, Lianyungang City, Jiangsu Province, China.

Founded with the mission of providing high-quality abrasive products to global customers, we have built a modern production base with advanced production equipment and strict quality control systems.

Our main products include silicon carbide (black and green), fused alumina (white and brown), abrasive tools, and refractory materials, which are widely used in grinding, polishing, sandblasting, refractory and other industrial fields.

With years of experience in the abrasive industry, we have established long-term cooperative relationships with customers from more than 30 countries and regions around the world.
